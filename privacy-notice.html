Your Personal Data Rights

Adhering to contemporary privacy norms and founded on principles of openness and responsibility, you are afforded several entitlements concerning your personal information:

  • Right to Access: You are entitled to ask for a copy of the personal data we maintain about you. Upon such a request, we will furnish you with your data along with details about how it is processed.
  • Right to Rectification: Should you find any of your personal information to be incorrect or incomplete, you have the right to have it corrected. You may seek to modify or update your data at any point.
  • Right to Erasure: Under specific conditions, you can request the removal of your personal data. Once a legitimate request is submitted and permissible by relevant laws, your data will be erased from our systems within the legally defined period.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: You might possess the right to limit, halt, or control the processing of your personal data in certain scenarios.
  • Right to Object: You can challenge the processing of your data for particular reasons, including direct marketing. If you choose to unsubscribe from marketing or adjust your communication preferences, we will honor your request promptly.
  • Right to Data Portability: Where technically feasible, you have the right to obtain your personal data in a structured, widely accepted, and machine-readable format for transfer to another data controller.

To invoke any of these rights, please reach out to us using the contact details provided in the concluding section of this document. All such requests will be managed in compliance with relevant legal provisions and within statutory timeframes.
