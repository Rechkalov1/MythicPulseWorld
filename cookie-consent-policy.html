Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small digital files, essentially text snippets, placed on your device by your web browser when you visit a website. These files contain data fragments that assist in identifying your unique browser or device, enabling a range of functions designed to enhance your online experience. While cookies themselves do not inherently store personal information, they can be utilized in conjunction with personal data you provide or data gathered during your visit, facilitating the tracking of your online activities or recalling your preferences for subsequent visits.

It is crucial to recognize that cookies empower website providers to deliver improved functionality and ease of navigation by retaining user settings, choices, and other data linked to your device or browsing session. This policy will clarify the different types of cookies we use, their specific objectives, and the methods available for you to manage or decline their use.
